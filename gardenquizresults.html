If your soil is polluted, use pots or raised flowerbeds for planting!

Do not grow any edible plants directly in the ground!

It's important to do a soil test before planting directly in ground,

especially if you plan on growing any edible plants!

 

You can get send your soil to the Brooklyn College Soil Lab to get tested for contaminants.

If unsure of your soil quality, use pots or raised flowerbeds for planting!
