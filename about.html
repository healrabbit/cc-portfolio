Hi! I'm Evie. I'm a Design and Technology student at Parsons School of Design. This is my portfolio project for my CC Lab Class.

I create art under the name "healrabbit". I have a fine arts background, but also have a keen interest in code and electronics.

I think what draws me to physical things like painting and digital things like code is the same. It all feels like a puzzle that needs to be solved. Oil painting from a reference, for instance, involves mixing the perfect colors and putting them in the correct places.

Coding is like that. Typing the right things and putting them in the right places. For me, creating provides a controlled enviornment when things feel out of control.
